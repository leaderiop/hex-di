================================================================================
                    RESOLUTION TRACING - TIMELINE VIEW DETAIL
================================================================================

The Timeline View shows resolution events as horizontal bars on a time axis.
Each bar represents one service resolution, with length proportional to duration.

TIMELINE ROW COMPONENT STRUCTURE:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

TimelineRow
  +-- OrderBadge (#1, #2, etc.)
  +-- PortNameLabel (clickable - expands to show details)
  +-- TimelineBarContainer
  |     +-- TimelineBar (styled div with dynamic width and position)
  |     +-- DurationLabel
  +-- LifetimeBadge (reuse from existing nodeStyles)
  +-- StatusIndicators
        +-- SlowIndicator (conditional)
        +-- CacheIndicator (conditional)

VISUAL STATES:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Bar Colors (using existing palette + new additions):

  Fast Resolution (< 10ms):
    - Bar:        #a6e3a1 (green, --hex-devtools-singleton)
    - Background: transparent

  Medium Resolution (10ms - threshold):
    - Bar:        #f9e2af (yellow, catppuccin yellow)
    - Background: transparent

  Slow Resolution (>= threshold):
    - Bar:        #f38ba8 (red, catppuccin red)
    - Background: rgba(243, 139, 168, 0.1) (subtle red glow)
    - Border:     1px solid #f38ba8 (on row container)

  Cache Hit (any duration):
    - Bar:        #89dceb (cyan, catppuccin cyan)
    - Icon:       [*] or filled circle
    - Background: transparent

Hover State:
  - Row background: var(--hex-devtools-bg-hover)
  - Bar:            slightly brighter (10% lighter)
  - Cursor:         pointer

Selected/Expanded State:
  - Row background: var(--hex-devtools-bg-secondary)
  - Left border:    3px solid var(--hex-devtools-accent)
  - Details panel:  slides down below bar

RESPONSIVE BEHAVIOR:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Narrow width (< 400px):
  - Hide duration labels on bars
  - Show duration in tooltip on hover
  - Reduce time ruler ticks

Wide width (> 600px):
  - Show full details inline
  - More time ruler granularity
