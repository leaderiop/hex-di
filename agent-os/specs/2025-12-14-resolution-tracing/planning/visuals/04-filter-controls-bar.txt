================================================================================
                    RESOLUTION TRACING - FILTER/CONTROLS BAR
================================================================================

The controls bar provides filtering, sorting, and threshold controls for
the Resolution Tracing section. It follows existing patterns from the
ResolvedServices component (search, filter buttons).
